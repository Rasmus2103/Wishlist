Q: How do I create a wishlist?

A: To create a wishlist, simply sign up for an account on our website, log in, and click on the "Create Wishlist" button. You can then add items to your wishlist by searching for them in our database or manually adding them using the "Add Item" feature. You can also customize your wishlist by setting a title, description, and cover image.

Q: How can I share my wishlist with others?

A: Once you've created a wishlist, you can share it with others by clicking on the "Share" button on your wishlist page. This will generate a unique link that you can send to your friends and family. You can also share your wishlist on social media platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, and Pinterest.

Q: Can I add items from any online store to my wishlist?

A: Yes, our wishlist web app supports adding items from any online store. You can either search for the item in our database and add it to your wishlist, or manually add the item by providing its name, image, and link. Our app will automatically generate a preview of the item with the image and link for easy access.

Q: How can I mark an item as purchased?

A: To mark an item as purchased, simply go to your wishlist page and click on the "Mark as Purchased" button next to the item. This will update the item status to "Purchased" and notify the wishlist owner that the item has been purchased. You can also add a personal note or leave a message for the wishlist owner when marking an item as purchased.

Welcome to Wishlist Creator

Wishlist Creator is a web app that lets you create and share wishlists for any occasion. Whether it's your birthday, a holiday, or just because, Wishlist Creator makes it easy to keep track of the things you want and share them with others.

Features

  • Create and manage multiple wishlists
  • Add items with images, descriptions, and links
  • Organize items into categories
  • Share wishlists with friends and family
  • Mark items as purchased
  • Customize wishlist settings and privacy
